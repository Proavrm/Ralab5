RaLab5 - Simple PreparationPage

Included
- frontend/react/src/pages/PreparationPage.jsx

What this version does
- Keeps preparation simple and focused on the future intervention
- Uses a minimal structure:
  1) Contexte
  2) Préparation de la mission
  3) Organisation
  4) Contraintes / remarques
- Preserves hidden legacy preparation fields when saving by merging:
  { ...originalPrep, ...form }
- Keeps module activation available
- Adds button:
  'Créer une intervention'
  which opens:
  /interventions/new?... with prefill query params

Important
- No modal
- No technicien field here
- No detailed essai logic here
- Keeps the idea:
  Préparation = organiser
  Intervention = exécuter
